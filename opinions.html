Opinions:

Here, you'll be presented with questions and asked to answer with the opinion that's closest to yours. When you click on an option for each question, you'll be taken to a page that briefly explains an opposing point of view to your own. This is meant to force you to reflect on your understanding of AI and consider opposing points of view.

Question 1: What is Artificial Intelligence?

a) A mechanism that can perform a specific task that humans do (ex. play chess, work an assembly line, etc.).

b) A thinking, conscious machine that is as good as or better than the human brain.
