Question 3: Should We Pursue Artificial Intelligence?

Your answer: Yes, the benefits for the human race outweigh the risks.

You have chosen to support AI development because you believe the benefits outweigh the risks.

The opposing view is that the risks far outweigh the possible benefits. Stephen Hawking and Elon Musk, some of the most respected minds in the world actually warn against the major risks of developing strong AI. The worry is that the AI will self-replicate, quickly surpassing humans and surpassing any limits we place on them to obey us. A higher form of intelligence might not value human life as we do or could see the horrible effects we are having on the environment.
